About PickleDink

PickleDink (PDINK) is the utility token that powers the PicklePayouts ecosystem, a platform for pickleball players to compete, wager, and earn rewards on the Solana blockchain.

PDINK is used for tournament entry fees, match wagering, earning rewards, marketplace purchases, and eventually community governance on the PicklePayouts platform.



  • First dedicated cryptocurrency for pickleball enthusiasts
  • Built on Solana for fast transactions and low fees
  • Multiple utilities within the gaming ecosystem
  • Community-driven development and governance
